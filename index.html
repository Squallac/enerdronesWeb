Our company provides powering solutions for drones, offering autonomous charging stations with wireless energy beaming technology. Enhancing drone autonomy, reducing idle time, and improving the capabilities of autonomous vehicles.

The opportunity

Widespread adoption of UAVs in different industries has been hampered by various factors, one of the most prevalent ones is the power source and its short endurance.

Current power sources are mostly Li-ion batteries, and although there have been efforts to “quick charge” them, it still takes too much time compared to alternatives like oil-powered UAVs.

That limitation is key for UAVs whose autonomy and “work time” directly depends on it, and one that if solved, could open a whole new arrange of capabilities.
