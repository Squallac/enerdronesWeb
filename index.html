We want to create an underlying infrastructure for drone operations to allow free movement of UAVs in cities and among cities, which will potentialize the adoption rate of drone technology and services in the transportation, security, logistics industries among others.

The opportunity

Batteries are the most prevalent problem for commercial drones, It’s low capacity limits in terms on range, operational time and payload; all essential metrics for businesses using or considering adopting drones

Our solution

Charging Station

EnerDrones charging system is a station for drones where to land and charge their batteries in a matter of minutes instead of hours. Sleek designed outdoors Energy beaming station using microwave technology to wirelessly fast-charge the drone’s battery.

Benefits

  • Wireless charging
  • Microwave energy beaming technology
  • Increases drone range, operational time and payload
  • Improves efficency and profitability

Energy absorption module

Module containing communications, charging control electronics and the nanotechnology rectenna to absorb the microwave energy beam.

Benefits

  • Charging battery 7x faster
  • High efficiency nanotechnology rectenna
